What's new

  • New School Canteen

    Ruby high school felt like making a canteen for the middle and high school students in the year 2019 because students who did not bring their tiffin boxes/ lunch boxes can eat from the canteen. Also in the classroom there will be no food scattered here and there after the reccess time. Our school will be maintained properly. We will not face any hygenic issue and also the children will enjoy eating from the canteen.

  • School Bus Location App

    Ruby High school decided to make a school bus location app as many parents were requesting to do so that they can see where the bus is going just for safety of their children. To login for the bus location, you need to first sign in with the following details: Both the parent's name, Child's name, bus number, Your building's name, Pin-code of the area you live in and phone number of both the parents.
